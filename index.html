Our Guiding Principle

Purpose

OMGC exists to provide a welcoming community where Malayalee seniors can stay connected, support one another, celebrate their heritage, and enjoy meaningful friendships and activities.

🔭

Vision

To be a vibrant and caring community where Malayalee seniors remain connected, valued, active, and enriched through friendship, fellowship, and shared cultural heritage.

Serviced & Supported

🎯

Mission

To bring Malayalee seniors together by fostering friendship, mutual support, lifelong engagement, and cultural connection through meaningful social, educational, cultural, and recreational activities.

Core Values

🤝

Friendship & Fellowship

Building lasting relationships and a sense of belonging.

❤️

Respect & Inclusiveness

Welcoming all members with dignity, respect, and understanding.

🫂

Service & Support

Caring for one another and strengthening our community through mutual support.

🎭

Culture & Heritage

Celebrating and preserving our Malayalee traditions, language, and values.

🌱

Active Living & Lifelong Learning

Encouraging participation, personal growth, health, and well-being.

Strategic Objectives

🔗

1. Connect

Strengthen social connections among members by providing regular opportunities to meet, interact, and build meaningful friendships through monthly gatherings and community events.

💛

2. Support

Promote the well-being of members by fostering a caring and inclusive community where individuals support one another through life's challenges and celebrate each other's milestones and successes.

🎉

3. Celebrate

Preserve and promote Malayalee culture, traditions, language, and heritage while creating opportunities to share and celebrate our identity across generations.

⚡

4. Engage

Encourage active and fulfilling living by organizing social, cultural, educational, and recreational activities that promote participation, learning, wellness, and enjoyment.

🌳

5. Grow

Ensure the long-term sustainability of OMGC by developing volunteers, planning leadership succession, and encouraging active member participation, while also strengthening relationships and collaboration with other Malayalee organizations to broaden community connections and impact.

Key Priorities 2026 – 2028

Concrete steps to build a stronger, more sustainable OMGC.

  • Expand wellness, educational, cultural, and social programming that reflects the interests and needs of members.
  • Increase member participation and engagement in meetings, events, programs, and community initiatives.
  • Strengthen volunteer involvement, leadership development, and succession planning to build organizational capacity.
  • Enhance governance and administrative effectiveness by registering OMGC as a nonprofit organization, adopting bylaws and policies, and strengthening board operations.
  • Preserve OMGC's legacy while improving communication, recognizing volunteer contributions, and developing partnerships and resources to ensure long-term sustainability.
  • Support steps — building stronger connections with other Malayalee organizations.

Our Guiding Principle

Connect • Support • Celebrate • Enjoy • Grow

Everything we do as an organization should help our members:

  • ✦ Stay connected and build meaningful friendships.
  • ✦ Support one another through life's journey in a caring community.
  • ✦ Celebrate our Malayalee heritage, culture, and the friendships we share.
  • ✦ Enjoy each other's company through meaningful, active, and fun-filled gatherings and activities.
  • ✦ Grow together by strengthening OMGC's sustainability, encouraging volunteerism and leadership, and building stronger connections with other Malayalee organizations.
