About Us

Our Story

OMGC was founded to create a supportive community for Malayalee seniors across Ontario. We provide a welcoming space where members can reconnect with their roots, build lasting friendships, and enjoy an active, fulfilling life.

🎯

Purpose

OMGC exists to provide a welcoming community where Malayalee seniors can stay connected, support one another, celebrate their heritage, and enjoy meaningful friendships and activities.

🔭

Vision

To be a vibrant and caring community where Malayalee seniors remain connected, valued, active, and enriched through friendship, fellowship, and shared cultural heritage. Serviced & Supported

🚀

Mission

To bring Malayalee seniors together by fostering friendship, mutual support, lifelong engagement, and cultural connection through meaningful social, educational, cultural, and recreational activities.

Strengthen social connections through regular meetings and community events.

💛

2. Support

Promote well‑being and mutual support through life’s challenges and celebrations.

3. Celebrate

4. Engage

5. Grow

Ensure sustainability through volunteer development, succession planning, and partnerships.
